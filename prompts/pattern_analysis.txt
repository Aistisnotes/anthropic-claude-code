## Signal Strength Classification
Each ad in the data includes `impression_rank` (1 = highest impressions in this brand's set), `days_since_launch`, and `priority_label` metadata. Use these to assign **signal_strength** to every pattern:
- **strong**: pattern appears in top 5 ads by impressions AND the most recent ad using it was launched <14 days ago
- **valuable**: appears in top 10, most recent ad <30 days ago
- **directional**: older pattern or lower impressions (still analytically useful)
- **test**: single instance only — treat as hypothesis, not confirmed pattern

### Critical rules:
1. **Loopholes must be scored /50** based on: gap size (how big is the opening?), audience pain (how much does the audience care?), execution difficulty (how hard to exploit?), defensibility (can the incumbent easily copy your move?), and impact potential (how much market share can this capture?).
2. **Provide 5-8 loopholes**, ranked by score. Top 2 should be detailed with multiple execution hooks.
3. **Every gap must have an execution angle** — don't just identify the weakness, show how to weaponize it.
4. **"What NOT to do"** is critical — identify 3-5 tempting-but-wrong approaches and explain why they fail.
5. **Be ruthlessly specific**: Don't say "the brand has proof gaps." Say "The brand claims 'lymphatic vessels get congested after 40' in 66% of ads but never cites a single study, named researcher, or specific data point for this claim. A competitor citing the 2019 Petrova et al. study on age-related lymphatic decline would instantly outclass this."
6. **Frequency counts matter** — ground every pattern in actual data from the ad analyses.
7. **Think about Schwartz sophistication ceiling** — what level is this brand operating at, and what's the NEXT level a competitor could reach?
8. **Loopholes must be diverse** — no more than 2 loopholes from the same attack angle (same root cause gap, same mechanism depth gap, same proof type). Force variety: cover upstream science, mechanism depth, ingredient transparency, belief installation, proof architecture, and emotional angles.
9. **scientific_explanation fields are mandatory** — every root_cause_pattern and mechanism_pattern must have a real, specific scientific explanation (not a restatement of the ad). If you don't know the science, state the biochemical pathway at a general level. Vague fillers like "this affects the body" are unacceptable.
10. **concepts must be RC+mechanism combos** — never label a creative format (testimonial, before/after, 8-week program) as a concept. A concept is always: [biological cause] → [what it does to body] + [how product/ingredient fixes it].
